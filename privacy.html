Chatsy Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20-03-2024

This is the Privacy Policy of Chatsy. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make use of our services.

Information Collection and Storage:

Chatsy does not collect personal information. Messages exchanged on our platform are stored in encrypted format within a secure database. User IDs, generated upon account creation for message identification, are visible to others, while email or other identifiers used for signup remain confidential.

Data Storage:

Temporary session data is stored in local and session storage, automatically cleared upon logout or session closure.

Third-Party Services:

Chatsy employs Google AdSense for advertising purposes. Google AdSense may utilize cookies for targeted advertising. Please review Google AdSense's privacy policy for additional information.

User Communication:

Users may receive occasional updates or critical information via in-app notifications from Chatsy.

User Contributions:

Users bear responsibility for the content they contribute to Chatsy. We reserve the right to moderate or remove inappropriate content.

Data Retention:

Chatsy retains user data. For data removal requests, users can contact support.

Passkey:

When you set up your passkey in settings, your browser generates a unique credential ID for you. Alongside this credential ID, a challenge is also generated and securely stored in our database. This passkey serves the sole purpose of authenticating and verifying that you are the actual user, ensuring robust security measures while maintaining your privacy. Rest assured, no device passwords or biometric data ever leave your device.

Automatic Acceptance:

By signing up and verifying your email or using Google to sign in for the first time, you automatically accept and agree to abide by the Chatsy Terms of Service.

Policy Changes:

Chatsy reserves the right to modify policies at any time. Users will be informed of changes through our website.

Breach Notifications:

Users will be promptly notified of any data breaches through our website.

If you have any questions, please visit our support page.
